Andradion Episode 2

 

In the small, lonely high school of Benicia, a man named Mr. Turner was at the peak of his career. He was a charismatic teacher, and every student looked foward to learning how to drive with him every year. One day, however, all that changed.

A race of aliens invaded Earth, and Benicia High School was their Terran Headquarters. To blend in, the aliens took the forms of human celebrities. Everybody was counting on his favorite teacher to solve the problem. Mr. Turner knew what he had to do.

Mr. Turner grabbed his pistol, and sped to school down the freeway at five miles per hour above the speed limit. Knowing the front entrance would be well-guarded, he entered through the side.

What stands ahead for Mr. Turner? What celebrity figures will he see? Press any key to begin.
